Italian Meat Charcuterie Board

A beautiful Italian Charcuterie board filled with Italian meats and cheeses and paired with sauces, fruits, and vegetables. Perfect to pair with your Italian meal.

Italian Meat Charcuterie Board

Prep Time—30 min

Total Time—30 min

Serves 8

Ingredients

  • Burrata or fresh mozzarella
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Brie or camembert
  • White sharp cheddar
  • Cured meats
  • Olives
  • Crackers and bread
  • Caprese Salad
  • Fruit
  • Nuts

Instructions

  1. On a large charcuterie board place the caprese along the side of the board. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  2. Place your small bowls with the olives.
  3. Arrange your meats on the board.
  4. Add in the cheese and dried fruit.
  5. Fill the rest of the board in with crackers, and nuts.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
