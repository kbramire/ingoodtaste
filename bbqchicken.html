BBQ Chicken Charcuterie Board

This fall charcuterie board full of seafood goodness is the perfect fall appetizer!

BBQ Chicken Charcuterie Board

Prep Time—35 min

Total Time—65 min

Serves 4—6

Ingredients

  • Brioche buns
  • Grilled corn on the cob
  • Red cabbage slaw
  • Potato salad
  • Mac and cheese
  • Roasted sweet potatoes
  • Baked beans
  • BBQ grilled chicken
  • Watermelon triangles
  • Strawberries
  • Dill pickle slices
  • Pickled red onions

Instructions

  1. Start by adding the beans, pickles, onions, potato salad, and coleslaw to separate small bowls. Place them in different corners of the board to anchor it.
  2. Next, add the chicken to the center of the board.
  3. Add the larger items, like corn, watermelon, and buns to the edge of the board.
  4. Fill in any holes with strawberries and sweet potatoes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
